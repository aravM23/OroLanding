Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026  ·  Effective: April 2, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Oro Digital Inc. ("Oro," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies on our website at buildingoro.ca. The Oro mobile application does not use cookies.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies are a feature of web browsers and are only relevant to our website (buildingoro.ca). The Oro mobile app does not use cookies — any data collected through the app is governed by our Privacy Policy.

2. Cookies We Use

Our website currently uses only essential cookies necessary for the website to function. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

Category Purpose Status
Essential cookies These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They are typically set in response to actions you take, such as submitting a form or setting your preferences. They do not store any personally identifiable information. In use
Analytics cookies Cookies that help us understand how visitors interact with our website, such as which pages are visited most often. We do not currently use these. Not in use
Marketing & advertising cookies Cookies used to track visitors across websites for advertising purposes. We do not currently use these. Not in use
Preference cookies Cookies that remember your settings and preferences to personalize your experience. We do not currently use these. Not in use

3. Essential Cookies in Detail

The only cookies currently set by buildingoro.ca are essential cookies required for basic website functionality. These may include:

  • Security cookies — used to protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF) and other security threats;
  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser, used to maintain your session while you navigate the site; and
  • Form state cookies — used to remember information you have entered in a form within your current session, so you do not have to re-enter it if you navigate away and return.

Because these cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function, they do not require your consent under applicable law.

4. Managing Cookies

Since we only use essential cookies, there is currently nothing to opt out of on our website. If we introduce non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate consent controls.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, refer to your browser's help documentation:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5. Future Changes

We may introduce analytics or other non-essential cookies in the future as our website grows. When we do, we will:

  • update this Cookie Policy to describe the new cookies in detail;
  • display a cookie consent banner on our website giving you the choice to accept or decline non-essential cookies; and
  • not set any non-essential cookies until you have given your explicit consent.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and post a notice on our website before the changes take effect. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
