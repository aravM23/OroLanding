2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in the following categories:

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed (encrypted) version of your password. We do not store your password in plain text.

Style preferences and wardrobe data

To provide personalized recommendations, we collect information about your style preferences, clothing items in your wardrobe (including descriptions, categories, and attributes), and outfit choices you make within the app.

Photos you upload

You may upload photographs of yourself or your clothing items. Before processing any photographs of you personally, we will request your explicit consent within the app. Once consent is given, we collect, store, and process those images using image recognition technology solely to assist with wardrobe organization and outfit recommendations. See Section 5 for details on how photos are shared with our AI service providers.

Style embeddings and preference data

Our AI systems generate mathematical representations of your style preferences (called "embeddings") based on your wardrobe data and interactions. These embeddings are stored in our database and are used to power your personalized recommendations. They are derived from your personal information and constitute personal information under applicable privacy legislation.

Feature usage data

We collect information about how you use the Service, including which features you access, how long you spend on different parts of the app, and interactions you make within the app. This data helps us understand how the Service is being used and improve the user experience.

Mailing list and waitlist data

If you signed up for our waitlist or mailing list, we hold your email address for the purpose of sending you updates about Oro. You may unsubscribe at any time. See Section 6 for details on our communications practices.

Technical and device data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the Service, including your device type and operating system, app version, general location (country/region level only, inferred from your IP address — not precise GPS location), and error and performance logs necessary to maintain and debug the Service.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect precise GPS location, payment card information, government-issued identification, or any biometric data beyond photographs you explicitly choose to upload.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected, or for compatible purposes you would reasonably expect. Specifically:

To provide the Service

We use your account information, wardrobe data, photos, and style embeddings to operate the Oro application and generate personalized outfit recommendations for you.

To improve and develop the Service

We use feature usage data and aggregated, anonymized information to understand how the Service is being used, identify bugs, and improve the user experience. Any use of your personal information to train or improve our AI systems requires your separate explicit consent, which we will request within the app.

To communicate with you

We use your email address to send transactional messages (account confirmations, security alerts, and service notifications) and, with your express consent, marketing and promotional communications about Oro.

To ensure safety and security

We use technical and usage data to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents, and to enforce our Terms of Service.

To comply with legal obligations

We may use or disclose your personal information where required to do so by applicable law, regulation, or court order.

4. Consent

We are committed to obtaining meaningful consent for the collection and use of your personal information, as required by the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Under PIPEDA, we are required to obtain your knowledge and consent before or at the time of collecting your personal information. We collect, use, and disclose your personal information only for the purposes described in this Policy. If we wish to use your personal information for a new purpose not identified in this Policy, we will identify that new purpose and obtain your consent before doing so.

How we obtain consent

We obtain your consent in the following ways:

  • At account creation — we present a clear summary of the personal information we collect and the purposes for which it will be used, and obtain your acknowledgment before completing registration;
  • At the point of photo upload — we present a clear, explicit consent request before processing any photographs of you; and
  • For AI training — if we wish to use your content to train or improve our AI systems, we will present a separate, explicit consent request within the app.

Withdrawing consent

You may withdraw your consent to any non-essential data processing at any time by contacting us at privacy@buildingoro.ca or through the settings within the app. Withdrawal of consent may affect our ability to provide certain features of the Service. Withdrawal of consent does not apply retroactively to personal information already collected and used in accordance with this Policy prior to withdrawal.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

AI and cloud service providers

To operate the Service, we share personal information with the following categories of third-party service providers, all of which are located in the United States:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers — we store all user data, including wardrobe data and photos, on servers provided by a major cloud infrastructure provider in the United States (currently AWS US-East-1, Northern Virginia);
  • AI processing services — to generate outfit recommendations and process wardrobe images, we send data including wardrobe descriptions and photographs to third-party AI service providers located in the United States. These providers are required by their terms of service and our data processing agreements to process your data only for the purposes of providing their services to us, and are prohibited from using your data to train their general AI models without your consent; and
  • Email service providers — we use third-party email delivery services to send transactional and marketing communications to you.

All third-party service providers with whom we share personal information are required to maintain appropriate safeguards and to use your information only for the specific purposes for which it was shared.

Brand partners

In future versions of the Service, we may share aggregated and anonymized analytics data with brand partners (such as the number of users who viewed a particular category of clothing). We will never share your personally identifiable information with brand partners without your explicit consent. We will update this Privacy Policy and provide appropriate notice before any such data sharing begins.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Oro, our users, or others.

Business transfers

If Oro is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or substantially all of its assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice within the app before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

7. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Oro is a Canadian company, but we use service providers located in the United States to operate our Service. This means your personal information is transferred to and processed in the United States, which has different privacy laws than Canada.

Specifically, your data is stored on servers located in the United States (AWS US-East-1, Northern Virginia), and is processed by AI service providers and email service providers also located in the United States.

While we take steps to ensure that our service providers maintain appropriate safeguards for your personal information, please be aware that your information may be subject to access by US government authorities under US law, including the USA PATRIOT Act and other national security legislation.

We inform you of these transfers through this Privacy Policy and through our in-app consent flows. By providing your personal information to us, you are acknowledging that it will be transferred to and processed in the United States as described in this Policy.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Our retention practices are as follows:

  • Photos — deleted within 30 days of your deletion request, including from our cloud storage and backup systems;
  • Account information and wardrobe data — retained for the duration of your account and deleted within 30 days of account closure;
  • Style embeddings — deleted within 30 days of account closure;
  • Feature usage logs — retained for up to 12 months, after which they are aggregated and anonymized or deleted;
  • Mailing list email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion; and
  • Technical and security logs — retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes.

After the applicable retention period, your personal information is securely deleted or anonymized so that it can no longer be associated with you.

Accuracy

We rely on you to provide accurate personal information and to update it when necessary. You may correct your account information at any time through the app settings or by contacting us at privacy@buildingoro.ca. We take reasonable steps to keep the personal information we hold as accurate and up-to-date as necessary for its stated purposes.

9. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • encryption of data in transit using TLS (Transport Layer Security);
  • encryption of data at rest in our cloud storage systems;
  • hashed storage of passwords — we never store your password in plain text;
  • access controls limiting who within Oro can access personal information; and
  • regular review of our security practices.

No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@buildingoro.ca.

In the event of a data breach that poses a real risk of significant harm to you, we will notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required by PIPEDA's breach notification rules.

10. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable privacy legislation, you have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Correction

Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information about you.

Deletion

Request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.

Withdrawal of consent

Withdraw your consent to non-essential data processing at any time.

Data portability

Complaint

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at www.priv.gc.ca or by calling 1-800-282-1376.
